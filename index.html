liminalismo is a shared linux system, which is sometimes called a pubnix or a tilde. this system is a tiny raspberry pi computer that physically exists somewhere in spain.

we exist as a community space for people who want to make a personal page, or quite simply poke around SSH and low-resource programming. we firmly endorse the slow web movement and want to provide a space for people to socialize asynchronously, to express themselves outside of the constraints that certain web formats put on our words, art, and thoughts.

To request an account, simply write an e-mail to tilde@liminalismo.com that contains:
  • your desired username. this will be the name with which you access to the server and the URL of your page.

  • your e-mail address. this is so that we can write back when your submission is approved, and to redirect your liminalismo mail to your main address.

  • a brief paragraph telling us why you want to join. this is both to prove you're a human and to make sure your projects are not too heavy for our resources.
